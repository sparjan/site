I'm a rising senior studying Math and Computer Science at Wellesley College. I'm passionate about exploring the role of autonomous agents in Earth system science and space exploration with a focus on navigation, perception, and planning/execution.

Questions that motivate me:

  • How do we build trust into autonomous systems? How do we preserve human oversight and engagement as the reach of automation expands?
  • As more and more complex Earth system data becomes available to us, how can we visualize it accessibly and translate it into actionable intelligence?
  • And broadly, what role do advanced technologies play in empowering how we learn, work, and develop sustainably?
